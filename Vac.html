The Cool Captain

MS Dhoni led India in 200 ODI matches, the most of any captain to have led Team India in ODI cricket. Out of a total of 200, team India won 100 and lost 74 with a win percentage of 55.
He has the most wins as an ODI captain when we compare his record with Azharuddin, Virat Kohli, and Sourav Ganguly.Previously Dhoni was the goalkeeper for his DAV Jawahar Vidya Mandir school's football team but after seeing his goalkeeping skills, coach Keshav Ranjan Banerjee, one who inspired Dhoni to be a cricketer, picked him to play cricket for his school team.
